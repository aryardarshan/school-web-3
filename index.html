Admissions

Thank you for your interest in applying to The Brigade Schools. The admission process at The Brigade Schools is a very simple one. For In-depth understanding of the admission procedure, please read the respective instructions very carefully. REGISTRATIONS ARE OPEN FOR THE ACADEMIC SESSION 2021-22 FROM MONDAY 21ST SEPTEMBER 2020. Seats are available in: JP Nagar School—Nursery, LKG, UKG, Class 3 to 9 & Class 11. Limited seats in Class 1 and 2 Mahadevapura School—Nursery to Class 8 Malleswaram School—Nursery, Class 7 to 9 The Application Process to The Brigade School begins with submitting a fully filled Expression of interest form. The Expression of Interest form can either be submitted On-line or you can download the Off-line form, print it, fill it and submit it at the school.
